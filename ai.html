What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is a way of making machines behave like human beings. It allows computers or robots to think, solve problems, and make decisions. The goal of AI is to create systems that can do tasks which usually need human intelligence — like understanding speech, recognizing images, or playing games.

Imagine a robot that can talk to you, answer questions, or help clean your house — that's AI in action. Even simple things like your phone’s auto-correct, Google Maps, or the way Netflix recommends movies — they all use AI.

Everyday Examples of AI
• Google Assistant or Siri: You ask a question, and they answer. That's AI!
• Face Unlock: Your phone recognizes your face and unlocks — this is AI using your image to make a smart decision.
• Email Spam Filter: AI automatically sends unwanted emails to the spam folder.
• Online Shopping Suggestions: When Amazon shows “You may also like this” — that is AI predicting your interests.
